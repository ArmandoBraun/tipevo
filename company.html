Back in 2016, at a pizza shop in West Hartford, CT, a group technologists, program administrators, parents, coaches and athletes set out on a mission — to help simplify, understand, and navigate the youth sports ecosystem across America.

Achieving this mission requires a sophisticated combination of innovative technology, communication solutions, and, most importantly, thinking about youth sports in a different way. It’s no doubt a tall order. But the good news is that much of the complexity which has transformed youth sports can be addressed and revolutionized by our company.

TiPEVO was established to streamline the ability to search, connect, and play, creating the first solution of its kind. It is one that has all the foundational aspects to establish a universal market standard and foster a new youth sports landscape that prioritizes transparency, collaboration, and communication.
